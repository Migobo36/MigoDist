# San Antonio Fuel Distribution Competitors - Market Analysis

## Direct Competitors (Same Service Category)

### 1. **Bear Oil**
- **Location**: San Antonio & Schertz, TX
- **Services**: Fuel & lubricant distribution, fuel cards, delivery, off-road diesel, on-site fueling
- **Customer Base**: Fleet operators, service businesses, equipment operators
- **Status**: Established regional player
- **Strengths**: Local presence, diverse fuel types, fuel cards program

### 2. **3L Energy Solutions**
- **Location**: San Antonio, TX
- **Services**: Bulk diesel, gasoline, industrial lubricants, DEF, fuel tank solutions (250-12,000 gallons)
- **Customer Base**: Fleets, contractors, generators, equipment operators
- **Contact**: 713-434-7600
- **Strengths**: Large-scale storage solutions, bulk capabilities

### 3. **Elan Fuels**
- **Location**: San Antonio (statewide coverage)
- **Services**: Fuel delivery for fleets, generators, equipment; on-site fueling, bulk options
- **Focus**: Fast on-site fueling with transparent pricing and local service
- **Customer Base**: Fleet operators, construction, industrial

### 4. **Anytime Fuel Pros**
- **Location**: San Antonio, TX
- **Services**: On-site reefer fueling, fleet diesel delivery
- **Specialization**: Mobile fueling for trucks, trailers, equipment
- **Focus**: Keeping fleets ready for the road

### 5. **American Petroleum LLC**
- **Location**: San Antonio, Austin, El Paso, and beyond
- **Services**: On-site fleet diesel fueling for logistics & transportation
- **Area Coverage**: Multi-city, regional expansion
- **Customer Base**: Trucking fleets, logistics companies

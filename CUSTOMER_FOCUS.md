## 💼 Why This Focus Works

**Ranparr's success** is built on serving the "sweet spot" of businesses:
- Large enough to have meaningful fuel budgets (worth the effort)
- Small enough to be relationship-focused (don't have corporate contracts)
- Growing and price-sensitive (can switch providers easily)
- Spread across many industries (lower customer concentration risk)

**Migo's applies this exact model** to San Antonio.

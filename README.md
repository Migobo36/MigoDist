## 🎯 Core Value Proposition

**"San Antonio's fuel partner for small businesses."**

- Guaranteed lowest prices (beat gas stations & competitors)
- Free fuel tanks + free maintenance
- Four fuel types: Unleaded, Premium, Red Diesel, Diesel
- No long-term contracts—month-to-month only
- Local, responsive San Antonio service

## 🎨 Design Approach

✓ **Clean, professional** — Burgundy (#8B3A3A) + cream (#FFD700) color scheme
✓ **Mobile responsive** — Scales perfectly on phone, tablet, desktop
✓ **Fast loading** — No heavy graphics, minimal CSS
✓ **Easy navigation** — Consistent nav bar across all pages
✓ **Action-oriented** — CTAs on every page pointing to contact
✓ **Customer-centric** — Every section focused on business owner pain points

## 📊 Design Philosophy

**Every word earns its place.** No fluffy corporate speak. Just:
- What you do
- Why it matters to them
- How to get started

Businesses don't need a novel—they need to know:
- **Do you save me money?** (Yes—guaranteed lowest prices)
- **Is it hassle-free?** (Yes—free tanks & maintenance)
- **Can I trust you?** (Yes—local, no-contract guarantee)
- **How do I get started?** (Call for a free quote)
